## Description

Alchono is a private space to understand your relationship with alcohol — and change it at your own pace.

No lectures. No judgement. No streak-shaming. Just honest tools that meet you where you are, whether that's cutting down, taking a break, or quitting entirely.

CHECK IN, IN SECONDS
One tap to log how you're feeling or that you're drinking. Alchono quietly builds the picture: your patterns, your triggers, your progress.

GET THROUGH URGES
When a craving hits, the app takes over: guided breathing, quick games to break the loop, and small real-world actions built around *your* life — message your partner, take the dog out, step outside. Then an honest question: did it pass?

TALK TO SOMEONE (HUMAN OR NOT)
An AI companion trained to listen without judging, available at 3am. And real mentors — people months or years into their own recovery — you can connect with one-to-one when you want a human who's been there.

A COMMUNITY THAT GETS IT
Share anonymously with people on the same road. Reactions only — no reply-guys, no judgement.

YOUR DATA IS YOURS
Everything is private by default and protected at the database level. Export all of it or delete your account and every trace of it — in the app, instantly, no emails to support.

DESIGNED FOR REAL LIFE
Multiple drinking sessions in a day happen. Morning reflections, not morning guilt. An honest "I'm going to drink anyway" button — because awareness counts, and we'll still be here.

Alchono is a self-help tool, not a medical service. If you experience severe withdrawal symptoms, seek medical help.
