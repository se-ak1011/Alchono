Privacy Policy

Alchono · Last updated 2 July 2026

Alchono helps people understand and change their relationship with alcohol. That means the data you trust us with is unusually personal, and we treat it that way. The short version: your data is yours, it is never sold, never used for advertising, and you can export or permanently delete all of it at any time, in the app, without asking us.

What we collect

  • Account: your email address and a username you choose.
  • Onboarding preferences (optional): things you choose to tell us so the app can support you better — for example the first names of people at home, whether you have pets, your working pattern, and your city. Every field is optional.
  • Your recovery data: mood check-ins, drinking sessions you log, journal entries, goals, community posts, and private messages you exchange with mentors or other members.
  • AI conversations: messages you send to the AI support companion.
  • Device data: a push notification token, if you enable notifications. We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics profiles.

How your data is used

  • To run the app: showing your own history, insights, and streaks back to you.
  • To personalise support: for example, suggesting you check in with the people you told us about during an urge.
  • To provide the AI companion: the content of your AI chat messages is sent to OpenAI to generate a response. OpenAI processes this under their API terms and does not use API data to train models. We send only the conversation text — never your name, email, or profile.
  • To keep the community safe: reports you submit are reviewed by our team.

What others can see

  • Community posts are visible to other signed-in users, and are anonymous by default.
  • If you become a mentor, your username, recovery length, and the bio you write are visible to other users. Nothing else.
  • Mentor messages are private between the two participants.
  • Your check-ins, sessions, journals, goals, and AI conversations are visible to you alone, enforced at the database level.

Where your data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (our database and authentication provider), protected by row-level security so each account can only access its own rows. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your rights and controls

  • Export: Profile → Export my data gives you a complete machine-readable copy.
  • Delete: Profile → Delete account permanently removes your account and every piece of data linked to it — check-ins, sessions, journals, posts, messages, AI conversations, everything. This is immediate and irreversible.
  • Block and report: you can block any user and report any post or message from inside the app.

If you are in the EU/UK, the above covers your GDPR rights of access, portability, and erasure. For anything else, contact us at the address below.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We never show your identity on anonymous posts — not to other users, not in reports.
  • We never use your recovery data for anything other than showing it back to you and making the app work.

Not medical advice

Alchono is a self-help and harm-reduction tool, not a medical service. The AI companion is not a therapist or doctor. If you are experiencing severe withdrawal symptoms or a medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.

Age

Alchono is for adults. You must be of legal drinking age in your country to use it.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will note it in the app before the change takes effect.
